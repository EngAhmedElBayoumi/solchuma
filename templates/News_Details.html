18 May 2023

Sipchem concludes its participation as a titanium sponsor in the 14th GPCA Supply Chain Conference.

As part of its continuous efforts to support the readiness of the petrochemical industry for the future, Sipchem concludes its participation as a titanium sponsor in the Gulf Petrochemicals and Chemicals Association (GPCA) Conference for Supply Chain in its fourteenth edition, which was held in Al-Khobar during May 16â€“17, 2023. The conference was attended by Vice President of Shared Services, Eng. Omar Bahobail, and the Sipchem supply chain team.

The conference included many workshops and discussion sessions in which several leaders in the fields of petrochemical industry, transportation, and logistics services participated, experiences were exchanged, and avenues of cooperation between companies in the field of supply chain were explored.

Among its other notable achievements, Sipchem received the "Excellence in Sustainability" award, this achievement was a clear outcome of Sipchem's commitment to environmental preservation as it has successfully converted their wooden Pallets; that are used for polymer product packaging, to Multi-used Recycled Plastic Pallets, in accordance with the sustainable business objectives of the Kingdom's Vision 2030.
